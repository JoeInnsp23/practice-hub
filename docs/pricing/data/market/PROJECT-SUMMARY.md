## üí° Key Findings & Insights

### Market Structure

**Pricing Model Distribution:**
- 78.3% use Model A (turnover-based pricing)
- 16.8% use Model B (transaction-based pricing)
- Model B commands **43.6% premium** over Model A

**Entity Type Pricing:**
- Ltd companies: ¬£139.68/month average
- Sole traders: ¬£62.22/month average
- **Ltd companies pay 124% more** than sole traders

**Market Segmentation:**
- Budget (¬£0-50): 43.2% of market
- Standard (¬£51-100): 29.0% of market
- Premium (¬£101-200): 18.6% of market
- Enterprise (¬£201+): 9.3% of market

**Top 3 Services (59.9% of dataset):**
1. COMP_ACCOUNTS (437 obs, ¬£135.50 avg) - Company accounts preparation
