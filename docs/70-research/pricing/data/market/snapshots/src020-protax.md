## Pricing Strategy Insights

### Value-Add Differentiation

**Observation:** Protax uses tax investigation insurance as primary differentiator:
- Mentioned on every page
- Included in all packages (no extra cost)
- Market value ~£150/year (builds perceived value)

**Application to Practice Hub:**
- Consider similar value-adds:
  - Free Xero/QuickBooks setup (£100-£200 value)
  - Free company formation (if we add this service)
  - Free first-year email support upgrades
  - Partnership with insurance providers

---

### Standard vs Premium Tiers

**Observation:** Protax offers two levels of service at each turnover band:
- Standard: Core compliance only
- Premium: Compliance + proactive tax planning + priority support

**Price Premium:** £150-£500 extra for Premium (20-30% markup)

**Application to Practice Hub:**
- Current approach: Single Model A price per turnover band
- Enhancement: Offer "Basic" vs "Premium" at each band
  - Basic: Model A price (accounts + CT only)
  - Premium: Model A price + 20% (adds SATR, quarterly calls, priority support)

---

### Landlord Specialization

**Observation:** Protax targets landlords with dedicated packages:
- Price per property decreases with volume (£133 → £108)
- Includes capital allowances advice (valuable for landlords)

**Application to Practice Hub:**
- Our rental addon: £30/property (annual)
- Consider dedicated landlord package (bundle accounts + SATR + rental addon)
- Marketing angle: "Landlord-friendly accounting"
