## Welcome

Thank you for choosing Innspired Accountancy! We're excited to work with you.

Before we can start providing services, UK law requires us to verify your identity and conduct basic compliance checks. This process is called **Know Your Customer (KYC)** and is required under the **Money Laundering Regulations 2017**.

**Good news**: The entire process takes about **10-15 minutes** and is completed online from the comfort of your home or office.

---

## Why Onboarding?

### Legal Requirements

UK law requires all accounting firms to:
- **Verify your identity** before providing services
- **Conduct AML (Anti-Money Laundering) checks** to prevent financial crime
- **Maintain records** of these checks for regulatory compliance

### What We Check

1. **Identity Verification**: Confirm you are who you say you are
2. **Document Verification**: Validate your ID document is genuine
3. **Facial Recognition**: Match your live photo to ID document
4. **AML Screening**: Check against government watchlists (standard procedure, not personal)

**Rest assured**: This is a legal requirement for all clients, not a reflection on you personally.

### Step 5: Identity Verification (LEM Verify)

**What is LEM Verify?**
- Our identity verification partner
- FCA-regulated and UK GDPR compliant
- Used by banks, solicitors, and accountants across the UK
- Conducts checks in real-time (2-5 minutes)

**What They Check**:

**1. Document Verification** (30 seconds)
- Is the document genuine?
- Is it valid and not expired?
- Does it match government databases?

**2. Facial Recognition** (1 minute)
- Take a selfie
- System compares your live photo to ID document photo
- Ensures you're the person on the ID

**3. Liveness Detection** (1 minute)
- Proves you're a real person (not a photo or video)
- May ask you to turn head, smile, or blink
- Prevents fraud

**4. AML Screening** (2 minutes)
- Check against UK and international watchlists:
  - **PEP**: Politically Exposed Persons
  - **Sanctions**: Government sanctions lists
  - **Watchlists**: Law enforcement watchlists
  - **Adverse Media**: Financial crime news

**How It Works**:

1. **Click "Start Verification"**
2. **Grant camera permission** (required for selfie)
3. **Review your ID document** (already uploaded)
4. **Take a selfie**:
   - Look directly at camera
   - Remove glasses and hats
   - Ensure good lighting
   - Keep face in frame
5. **Complete liveness check**:
   - Follow on-screen instructions
   - May ask you to turn head left/right
   - May ask you to smile or blink
   - Takes 30-60 seconds
6. **Wait for results** (1-2 minutes)

**Tips for Successful Verification**:
- ‚úÖ Use a well-lit room (natural light best)
- ‚úÖ Look directly at camera
- ‚úÖ Keep face in frame
- ‚úÖ Follow instructions carefully
- ‚úÖ Stay still during checks
- ‚ùå Don't wear sunglasses or hats
- ‚ùå Don't use filters or beauty modes
- ‚ùå Don't cover your face

**What if verification fails?**
- Common causes: Poor lighting, blurry photo, incorrect document
- You can retry immediately (up to 3 attempts)
- After 3 attempts, contact support for assistance

### Troubleshooting Verification Issues

**"Document verification failed"**
- **Cause**: Document not recognized or appears invalid
- **Solution**:
  - Ensure document is genuine and current
  - Take better quality photo (see photo tips above)
  - Try different lighting
  - Try scanning instead of photo
  - Try different document (passport vs licence)

**"Facematch failed"**
- **Cause**: Your current appearance doesn't match ID photo
- **Solution**:
  - Remove glasses if you weren't wearing them in ID photo
  - Ensure good lighting on face
  - Look directly at camera
  - Try neutral expression
  - If ID photo is very old, consider renewing ID and re-verifying

**"Liveness check failed"**
- **Cause**: System couldn't confirm you're a real person
- **Solution**:
  - Ensure you're not in front of bright window (backlighting)
  - Improve room lighting
  - Hold device steady
  - Follow instructions precisely
  - Complete full range of motion for each instruction

**"AML screening requires review"**
- **Cause**: Your name matches (or is similar to) someone on watchlist
- **Solution**:
  - Don't worry - this is often a false positive
  - Our team will review manually
  - You may be asked to provide additional information
  - Usually resolved within 24 hours

**Still having issues?**
- Contact support: support@innspiredaccountancy.com
- Include screenshot of error message
- Mention what you've already tried
- We're here to help!
